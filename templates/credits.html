About

The FirewallReviews system is an advanced auditing framework developed by Information Technology students from the Pamantasan ng Lungsod ng Maynila (PLM). This platform is designed to streamline network security compliance by automating rule analysis against international cybersecurity benchmarks, including NIST SP 800-41 and ISO/IEC 27001 standards.
